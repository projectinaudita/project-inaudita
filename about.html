Our Essence

Project Inaudita is a cinematic experience, an interactive exploration, and a social wake-up call. We craft haunting short films and immersive ARGs that challenge societal apathy toward mental health and trauma. Our narrative-driven approach unveils the unspoken, the suppressed, and the forgotten — bringing light to the shadows where real lives suffer in silence.

Our Mission

We aim to dismantle the illusion that acknowledging mental health is enough. Through storytelling and alternative reality engagement, we reveal how societal structures praise awareness but fail in support. Inaudita is a voice for those whose cries were dismissed, and a mirror held to a world that too often looks away.

Why It Matters

Every piece of content, every coded message, every eerie interaction is part of a larger truth — that invisible scars are real. Our project is not just art; it's advocacy. It's not just entertainment; it's exposure. We want our audience to feel disturbed, awakened, and responsible.

Our Impact

Project Inaudita has made a significant local impact, reaching over 300 students through school sessions, peer discussions, and informal meetups, sparking vital conversations about trauma, emotional neglect, and mental health. Many individuals, particularly teens, have shared their personal stories for the first time and were connected to trusted support systems. Feedback from early testers of our interactive ARG has been overwhelmingly positive, with participants finding it both eerily personal and emotionally eye-opening. Our upcoming short film series has garnered interest from teachers, classmates, and local creatives, with some offering to contribute real-life experiences. We’ve also seen a noticeable shift in behavior, with more empathy, check-ins, and a growing willingness to challenge the stigma surrounding mental health. Having focused on real, face-to-face impact, we now have the opportunity to expand globally through digital storytelling, and we believe this is the perfect moment to take Project Inaudita to the world.
