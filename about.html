Inside Project Inaudita

Where silence breaks, stories unfold, and society is forced to confront what it ignores.

Our Essence

Project Inaudita is a cinematic experience, an interactive exploration, and a social wake-up call. We craft haunting short films and immersive ARGs that challenge societal apathy toward mental health and trauma. Our narrative-driven approach unveils the unspoken, the suppressed, and the forgotten — bringing light to the shadows where real lives suffer in silence.

Our Mission

We aim to dismantle the illusion that acknowledging mental health is enough. Through storytelling and alternative reality engagement, we reveal how societal structures praise awareness but fail in support. Inaudita is a voice for those whose cries were dismissed, and a mirror held to a world that too often looks away.

Why It Matters

Every piece of content, every coded message, every eerie interaction is part of a larger truth — that invisible scars are real. Our project is not just art; it's advocacy. It's not just entertainment; it's exposure. We want our audience to feel disturbed, awakened, and responsible.
