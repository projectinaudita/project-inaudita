How to Begin

You’re not lost. Not really. Confused? Probably. But you’re exactly where you need to be.

This is not a puzzle to be solved. It’s a story you’ve already stepped into. A truth that’s been buried, waiting for you to listen. To notice. To remember.

“Some scars aren’t visible... but they never really fade.”

Need a Nudge?

  1. Every glitch is intentional.
    If something breaks, stutters, or warps — that’s the point. Lean into it.
  2. Page names matter.
    Some paths are hidden in plain sight. Try broken links, strange URLs, misspelled words.
  3. Listen to the silence.
    If a page feels empty, try waiting. Refresh. Something always watches.
  4. Stories are keys.
    What others share on the Wall might unlock new paths. Pay attention to patterns and repeated phrases.
  5. You're not alone.
    If the messages feel personal, it’s because they are. Someone’s trying to reach you. Maybe it’s… you.
  6. When in doubt, break things.
    Highlight the text. Click what shouldn’t be clickable.
  7. Every phase has a heart.
    What is this part of the story really about? Guilt? Betrayal? Loneliness? Follow the feelings.
  8. Patience is the key to success.
    A wise person in my life once told me that.
  9. When can't find anything.
    Look closer. It's like a convex mirror in cars.
  10. Its a story.
    Sit back and enjoy until you can. ;)
